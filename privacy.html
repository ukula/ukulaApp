Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Ukula is a lightweight OCR translation utility for Windows. The application does not require user accounts and does not build personal user profiles.

Translation and OCR Processing

When using OCR or translation features, selected text or captured screen regions may be processed through third-party translation providers chosen by the user.

Supported providers may include:

  • Google Translate
  • DeepL
  • Microsoft Azure Translator

Translation requests are sent directly to the selected provider using API credentials supplied and managed by the user.

Local Storage

Application settings such as language preferences, hotkeys, and interface options are stored locally on your device.

Ukula does not maintain cloud-based user accounts or remote user databases.

API Keys and Security

Any API credentials entered into the application are stored locally using your operating system's secure credential storage mechanisms, such as Windows Credential Manager.

Ukula does not collect, upload, sell, transmit, or share your API keys with external servers controlled by the developer.

Your credentials remain under your control and are used only to communicate directly with the translation providers you choose to use.

No Analytics or Telemetry

Ukula does not use analytics platforms, telemetry systems, advertising SDKs, behavioral tracking, or background user profiling technologies.

No Cloud Synchronization

Ukula does not require account registration and does not synchronize personal data to cloud services operated by the developer.

Third-Party Services

Users are responsible for reviewing the privacy policies, pricing, and terms of any third-party translation providers they choose to use with Ukula.

The developer is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Google, Microsoft, DeepL, or other third-party providers referenced by the application.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future to reflect application changes, feature additions, or legal clarifications.
